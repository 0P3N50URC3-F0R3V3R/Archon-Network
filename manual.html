THE LIGHT AND THE DARK — PLAYER MANUAL

HOW TO WIN

Capture all 5 Power Points simultaneously — or destroy every enemy piece.

Power Points are the glowing squares at the center and four cardinal positions of the board. Holding all five at the start of your turn wins the game instantly.

THE BOARD

The board is 9×9. Squares are either Light, Dark, or Charmed (the center cross pattern). Pieces cannot share a square — moving onto an enemy triggers combat.

Power Points

  • Center (4,4), Top (4,0), Bottom (4,8), Left (0,4), Right (8,4)
  • Pieces on power points cannot be targeted by most spells
  • Combat on a power point grants an HP bonus to the defender
  • Holding a power point heals the piece there at the start of each cycle

THE LIGHT CYCLE

The Light Cycle rotates through 6 phases (0–5) and shifts by one each full round. It determines which charmed squares favor which side:

  • Phase 3 — neutral (game start default for light-first)
  • Phases 4–5 — charmed squares favour Light
  • Phases 0–2 — charmed squares favour Dark

At phase 0 or 5 the imprisoned pieces of the favoured side are freed and healed.

MOVEMENT RULES

  • GROUND pieces move in cardinal directions only, one step at a time
  • FLY pieces move freely in any direction within their step limit
  • MAGE pieces (Wizard / Sorceress) teleport anywhere within range
  • You cannot pass through or land on your own pieces
  • Landing on an enemy square triggers combat (you choose whether to challenge)

COMBAT

When a piece moves onto an enemy square, real-time combat begins in a small arena with barriers.

  • Both pieces fight until one (or both) HP reaches zero
  • Damage carries over as injuries — a piece that survives many fights gets weaker
  • Fighting on your side's charmed square grants an HP bonus (damage reduction)
  • Power point squares also grant a bonus to the defending piece
  • If both pieces die simultaneously, both are removed
  • The winner occupies the contested square
  • Elementals are always removed after combat
TIP: Never challenge a Dragon on a dark square unless you have an overwhelming advantage. Retreat and use Imprison or fight on a charmed light square instead.

SPELLS (Wizard / Sorceress)

To use a spell: select your mage → press Enter on the mage's own square → Up/Down to browse → Enter to cast.

Each spell can only be used once per game. Pieces on power points are immune to most spells.

STRATEGY TIPS

  • Control power points early — even holding 3 creates pressure
  • Protect your Mage. Dead mage = no spells for the rest of the game
  • Imprison the enemy mage first if you can — it prevents spells for a turn
  • Use the Shapeshifter against the Dragon — it copies the Dragon's own stats
  • Fight on charmed squares of your colour for an HP advantage
  • Heal injured high-value pieces (Dragon, Golem, Djinni) before they die
  • Shift Time when the cycle is at phase 0 or 1 (dark territory) to flip it back
  • Knights and Goblins are expendable — use them to scout and probe defenses
